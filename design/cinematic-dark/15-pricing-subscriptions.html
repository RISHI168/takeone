Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the changes take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the changes take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more
We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, as well as PayPal. Enterprise clients can also pay via invoice or wire transfer.

Is there a student discount?

expand_more
Absolutely! Students with a valid .edu email address or student ID are eligible for a 50% discount on the Pro plan. Contact our support team to apply.

How does the free trial work?

expand_more
The free trial gives you full access to the Pro plan features for 14 days. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or automatically downgrade to the free Starter plan.
